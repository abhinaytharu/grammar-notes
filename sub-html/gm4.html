📘 Present Simple vs Present Continuous

🔹 1. Present Simple Tense (साधारण वर्तमानकाल)

✅ Structure:
Subject + V¹ (base form or +s/es)

🕒 Used for:

Condition Example
Regular actions / habits She drinks tea every day.
General truths / facts The sun rises in the east.
Timetables / schedules The train leaves at 5 PM.
Permanent situations He lives in Kathmandu.
Feelings, thoughts, states I know the answer.

🔹 2. Present Continuous Tense (अपूर्ण वर्तमानकाल)

✅ Structure:
Subject + is/am/are + V¹ + ing

🕒 Used for:

Condition Example
Actions happening right now She is reading a book.
Temporary actions / ongoing situations I am staying with my cousin.
Changing or developing situations The weather is getting colder.
Future plans (near future) We are going to the cinema.

🔁 Comparison Table

Condition Use Present Simple Use Present Continuous
Routine or Habit ✔ (I walk every day.) ✘ (Not used for routines)
Happening right now ✘ ✔ (She is eating now.)
Permanent situation ✔ (He works at a bank.) ✘
Temporary situation ✘ ✔ (He is working here for 2 weeks.)
Scheduled future (like timetables) ✔ (School starts at 10 AM.) ✘
Planned near future ✘ ✔ (I am meeting him tomorrow.)
General truth or fact ✔ (Water boils at 100°C.) ✘
Changing trends / developments ✘ ✔ (Prices are rising.)
